Title                   : Zorf
Filename                : ZORF1.WAD
Author                  : Ron Olsen
Email Address           : rolsen@nyx.cs.du.edu
Misc. Author Info       : Programmer

Description             : A constant action DEATHMATCH 2.0 level.

This level was designed for constant action, lots of running and chasing,
and lots of offense.  The intention was to eliminate ambush spots, and
safe spots, and keep the game moving at a fast pace.

Additional Credits to   : Little Gary Hintermeier  (ghinterm@nyx.cs.du.edu)
