To play this new level: "Maniac Operation" (E1M1).
- Copy MANIAC.WAD into the DOOM directory.
- Enter this line in DOS prompt: DOOM -file MANIAC.WAD
- Choose "New Game" in the principal menu.
- Select "Knee-Deep in the Dead" episode.

Please note that the skill level doesn't 
change anything, no pity!
You must survive... like a real man (or woman)!

You must have DOOM Version 1.2 Registered.
