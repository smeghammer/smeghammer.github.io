Title                   : Mangle   
Filename		: Mangle.wad
Author                  : Danny Hyde
Email Address           : send c/o rhyde@mirror.det.mun.ca

Description             : After restoring partial peace on earth,
			you make your way back home to find your 
			family has been captured by terrorists.
			You go to the terrorists base to rescue 
			your family. Before you even get in, the
			terrorists capture you. They tell you
			that you will not see your family again 
			unless you make it through Mangle. If you
			don't make it through, they will kill
			your family.
