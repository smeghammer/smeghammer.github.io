Title                   : Crater Lake
Filename                : LAKE.WAD
Author                  : Chuck Jordan
Misc. Author Info       : Mary Shelley wrote Frankenstein as a
			  bet with Lord Byron	

Description             : A deathmatch WAD for E1M1
			  The scene is a toxic lake in a crater
			  on Mars.  All the boathouses and 
			  control centers have been abandoned.
			  Keep on the bridges; if you fall off,
			  try to make it to a boat fast!
