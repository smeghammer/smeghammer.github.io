Description           : Alien forces have taken over an old installation
			and activated an nuclear reactor that should have
			been dismantled because of its instability. It it
			vital that the reactor is shut down.
			A slight problem with navigation upon landing on
			the site renders the taskforce with only one man,
			namely (you guessed it!) you!
