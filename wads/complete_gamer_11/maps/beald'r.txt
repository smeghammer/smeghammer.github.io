Installation
------------
Unzip the map and place it into your maps directory under the unreal
   directory. Type in "open <mapname>" to run the map. Alternatively,
   double-click the map.

Click on Beald'r.UNR. This runs the beginning map. 


Extended description:
--------------------

You've been stationed as a rebel, your mission is to free Beald'r Keeep from the clutches of evil! The Nali will be happy if you kill him.
