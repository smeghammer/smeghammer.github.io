* Play Information *
Its simple. You are marooned on Stroggos at the foot of a blockade. You can either pitch a tent or go down fighting. 



Single Player           : yes
Cooperative             : yes (but not recommended or tested)
Deathmatch              : no (I will be releasing a proper DM level based on this map)
Difficulty Settings     : Yes
New Sounds              : No
New Graphics            : No
