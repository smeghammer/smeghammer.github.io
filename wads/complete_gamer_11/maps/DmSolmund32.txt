Installation
------------
Unzip DmSolmund32.unr into the \maps folder under the Unreal folder.
Unzip faenta.umx into the \music folder under the Unreal folder.

------------------------------------------
to allow more than 16 players do this:
open unreal.ini in your unreal\system directory

look where it says:

[UnrealI.DeathMatchGame]

copy this text ower the old one:
 
MaxPlayers=32
InitialBots=31
