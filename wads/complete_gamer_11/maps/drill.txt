*Mission*

Search for your missing comrades and kick some ass along the way.

My farewell Q2 map before i move to Finland to work on Max Payne with
Remedy Entertainment. This took about 4 days to make, its a little short
but hopefully brutal enough :o) Its a little rushed, arghhhhhhh ;]
 

Just stick it on your quake2/baseq2/maps directory and at the console
type map drill
