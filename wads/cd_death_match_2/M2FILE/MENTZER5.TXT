Title                   : Mentzer5.WAD  (For Doom 2) 
Author                  : David J. Hill   
Email Address           : Mentzer@aol.com

Description             : AKA: Tunnels of Death

			  Another deathmatch wad in the Mentzer series.
			  Four tunnels connect all areas of this wad together.
			  Designed for dog fights over valuable territory.
			  All weapons are available, along with the megashere
		   	  and other goodies.  

			  Search aaaaaand ....seek and destroy!!!
