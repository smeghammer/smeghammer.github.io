Welcome

Welcome to my Doom site.

I thought I'd take advantage of the Github Pages feature for my Doom stuff repo and add a website front-end.

I'm a 50-something father of 2, metalhead biker who has been playing Doom on and off since the shareware release first came out in 1993... I have tinkered with level-editing a little back in the day, but have not done any for about 25 years until I started again this year when furloughed due to the COVID-19 crisis.

I am a coder by trade and after discovering the possibilities of Decorate, ACS etc. for extending and customising the base functionality of doom (via (G)ZDoom, I was keen to experiment with such features.

This site therefore presents my Doom map making adventures...
