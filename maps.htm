Maps

I started mapping again in April 2020, just after the whole COVID-19 thing started and I was put on furlough from my work.

Here you will find links to my completed maps, with screenshots.

Hellbreach

My first mapset, currently at 3 completed and one in progress.

This mapset is quite ambitious for a first attempt at mapping as each map is pretty large and complex. These were (and still are) a vehicle for learning to map... I will revisit this periodically and add more stuff.

I have released a version 1 of the first three maps as stand-alone WADs, each in the MAP01 slot. A full mini-episode will come in due course.
